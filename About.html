Our Mission

Beyond Dope Productions envisions a world beyond overdose deaths. Through resource education and awareness and providing a space for genuine and deep interpersonal connections, we seek to empower individuals with opiate addictions, so they can reduce the risks associated with active opiate abuse, and ultimately choose an opiate-free lifestyle. We strive to transform the bass music scene into a loving and supportive community for those in active addiction, recovery, and for all who have been impacted by opiate abuse.
